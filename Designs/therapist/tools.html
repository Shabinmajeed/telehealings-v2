Standardized instruments for patient evaluation and monitoring
PHQ-9 Depression Scale
The Patient Health Questionnaire-9 is a widely used 9-item instrument for screening, diagnosing, and monitoring the severity of depression.
GAD-7 Anxiety Scale
The Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-item scale is a validated self-report tool for measuring anxiety symptom severity in clinical practice.
Sleep Quality Index
The Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index assesses sleep quality and disturbances over a one-month interval to guide treatment planning.
