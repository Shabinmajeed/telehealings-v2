Small text uses 13px for meta labels, helper text, and less important details.

Accessibility

Modern interface design should also be inclusive. These rules help ensure the experience works for everyone.

  • Keep text contrast high enough for readability (minimum 4.5:1 ratio).
  • Use focus-visible outlines for keyboard users.
  • Keep interactive targets at least 44px tall.
  • Use semantic HTML and clear labels for form controls.
  • Provide sufficient color contrast for all states (hover, active, disabled).
  • Support screen readers with proper ARIA labels and roles.
